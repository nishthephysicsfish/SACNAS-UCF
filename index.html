SACNAS @ UCF

Society for Advancement of Chicanos/Hispanics & Native Americans in Science
Join us in achieving true diversity in STEM!

SACNAS @ UCF unites students, faculty, and professionals dedicated to supporting and advancing underrepresented communities in science. Through mentorship, professional development, outreach, and community building, we work to foster inclusion and equitable access to STEM opportunities for all.
  • Connect with peers, mentors, and allies passionate about diversity in STEM.
  • Participate in workshops, outreach events, and professional development activities.
  • Access conference funding, research opportunities, and networking resources.
  • Advocate for equity, inclusion, and representation in science and academia.
  • Build lifelong skills and community as you prepare for future careers and leadership roles.
